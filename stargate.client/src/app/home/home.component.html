Astronaut Career Tracking System (ACTS)

Welcome to the Astronaut Career Tracking System (ACTS)! ACTS is developed under Stargate, is designed to maintain a comprehensive record of those who have contributed to human space exploration. ACTS provides a structured system for tracking Ranks, Titles, and Duty Assignments throughout their careers.

On our directory page you will find a list of people, and can drill down further to see their details, and info regarding their career.
